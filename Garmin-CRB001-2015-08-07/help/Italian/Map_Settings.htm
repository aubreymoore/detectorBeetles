Map Settings

Informazioni mappa: Enables or disables the maps currently loaded on the device.

Orientamento: Adjusts how the map is shown on the page. Nord in alto shows North at the top of the page. Traccia in alto shows your current direction of travel toward the top of the page. Modalità Autoveicolo shows an automotive perspective with the direction of travel at the top.

Display: Sets a dashboard to display on the map. Each dashboard shows different information about your route or your location.

Testo di guida: Sets when the guidance text is shown on the map.

Velocità mappa: Adjusts the speed at which the map is drawn. A faster map drawing speed decreases the battery life.

Impostazione avanzata

Dettaglio: Sets the amount of detail shown on the map. Showing more detail may cause the map to redraw more slowly.

Rilievo ombreggiato: Shows detail relief on the map (if available) or turns off shading.

Veicolo: Sets the position icon, which represents your position the map. The default icon is a small blue triangle.

Controlli zoom: Sets the auto zoom and the location of the buttons in portrait or landscape mode.

Livelli zoom: Adjusts the zoom level at which map items will appear. The map items will not appear when the map zoom level is higher than the selected level.

Dimensioni testo: Sets the text size for map items.
