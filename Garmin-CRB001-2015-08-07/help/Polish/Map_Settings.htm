Map Settings

Konfiguruj mapy: Enables or disables the maps currently loaded on the device.

Orientacja: Adjusts how the map is shown on the page. Północ u góry shows North at the top of the page. Kierunek u góry shows your current direction of travel toward the top of the page. Tryb samochodu shows an automotive perspective with the direction of travel at the top.

Pulpit: Sets a dashboard to display on the map. Each dashboard shows different information about your route or your location.

Tekst wskazówek nawigacyjnych: Sets when the guidance text is shown on the map.

Prędkość rysowania mapy: Adjusts the speed at which the map is drawn. A faster map drawing speed decreases the battery life.

Zaawansowana konfiguracja

Szczegółowość: Sets the amount of detail shown on the map. Showing more detail may cause the map to redraw more slowly.

Cieniowany relief: Shows detail relief on the map (if available) or turns off shading.

Pojazd: Sets the position icon, which represents your position the map. The default icon is a small blue triangle.

Sterowanie powiększeniem: Sets the auto zoom and the location of the buttons in portrait or landscape mode.

Stopnie zoomu: Adjusts the zoom level at which map items will appear. The map items will not appear when the map zoom level is higher than the selected level.

Rozmiar tekstu: Sets the text size for map items.
