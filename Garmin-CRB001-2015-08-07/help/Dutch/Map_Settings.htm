Map Settings

Informações do mapa: Enables or disables the maps currently loaded on the device.

Orientação: Adjusts how the map is shown on the page. Norte no topo shows North at the top of the page. Trajeto para cima shows your current direction of travel toward the top of the page. Modo automóvel shows an automotive perspective with the direction of travel at the top.

Painel de instrumentos: Sets a dashboard to display on the map. Each dashboard shows different information about your route or your location.

Texto de orientação: Sets when the guidance text is shown on the map.

Velocidade do mapa: Adjusts the speed at which the map is drawn. A faster map drawing speed decreases the battery life.

Configuração avançada

Detalhe: Sets the amount of detail shown on the map. Showing more detail may cause the map to redraw more slowly.

Relevo sombreado: Shows detail relief on the map (if available) or turns off shading.

Veículo: Sets the position icon, which represents your position the map. The default icon is a small blue triangle.

Botões de zoom: Sets the auto zoom and the location of the buttons in portrait or landscape mode.

Níveis de zoom: Adjusts the zoom level at which map items will appear. The map items will not appear when the map zoom level is higher than the selected level.

Tamanho do texto: Sets the text size for map items.
